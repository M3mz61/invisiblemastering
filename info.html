Protocol

Invisible Mastering Inc provides world-class sonic finishes, ensuring your translations remain pristine across high-end sound systems, car stereos, and mobile devices alike.

Every master includes two complementary rounds of adjustments to guarantee your complete artistic vision is achieved. Files are delivered in pristine 24-bit WAV format alongside optimized files for streaming.

Delivery Specs

Please export files at their native sample rate, leaving at least -3dB to -6dB of headroom on the master bus. Ensure no heavy brickwall limiters or clipping plug-ins are active on your final export track output.
